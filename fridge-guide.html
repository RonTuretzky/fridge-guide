Fridge Guide

Label it or lose it — keep our kitchens clean and organized

🏷️ Label Every Item
  1. 1 Grab tape + permanent marker
  2. 2 Write your name and date (MM/DD)
  3. 3 Stick the label where it's easy to see

🗑️ Weekly Fridge Cleanout
  1. 1 Every Friday — all fridges are checked
  2. 2 Items older than 3 days get tossed
  3. 3 No label = thrown out immediately

❄️ Freezer Protocol
  1. 1 Same label rules — name + date
  2. 2 Max storage: 3 months
  3. 3 Use freezer-safe containers or bags
  4. 4 Don't overpack — air needs to circulate
  5. 5 Cleanout: 1st Friday of each month
